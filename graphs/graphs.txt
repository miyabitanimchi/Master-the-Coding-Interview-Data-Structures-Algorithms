=== Graphs ===
- One of the most usuful and used data structures in computer science when it comes to modeling real life
- It's a connection with diffenrent nodes
- Each item is called a node or a vertex
- Each node is connected with each other with edges
- Trees are types of graph

=== Types of Graphs ===
- Directed (One way) and Undirected Graphs
  - FB... Undirected, Twitter... Directed
- Weighted and Unweighted Graphs
 - Edges can also have information, and it's a weighted graph
- Cyclic and Acyclic

=== Directed Acyclic Graph ===
- commonly called DAG
- Iota, which is a popular blockchain project uses the graph
